# SEO Daily Ops — UAEBizConnect

## Daily (10–15 min)
- [ ] Homepage loads (200)
- [ ] `/robots.txt` loads (200)
- [ ] `/sitemap.xml` loads (200)
- [ ] Search Console: no new critical errors
- [ ] Submit newly published URLs for indexing

## Weekly (30–45 min)
- [ ] Review queries in positions 6–20
- [ ] Improve page titles/meta for low CTR pages
- [ ] Add internal links to new/priority pages
- [ ] Refresh at least 1–2 pages with better copy/FAQ
- [ ] Confirm sitemap includes all new URLs

## Monthly (60–90 min)
- [ ] Review keyword ranking movement
- [ ] Check backlink growth/referring domains
- [ ] Refresh top pages with current month/year context
- [ ] Prune/merge thin or redundant pages

## KPI Targets (90 days)
- [ ] 10+ long-tail keywords in top 10
- [ ] 3–5 high-intent keywords in top 5
- [ ] Consistent non-brand traffic growth
