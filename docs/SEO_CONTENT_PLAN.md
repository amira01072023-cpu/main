# SEO Content Plan — UAEBizConnect (Starter)

Use this as a structured template, then replace with UAE-specific keyword mapping and page briefs.

## Priority Content Clusters

1. Core directory intent pages
- UAE business directory
- Dubai business directory
- Abu Dhabi business directory

2. Category pages (high intent)
- Restaurants in Dubai
- Clinics in Abu Dhabi
- Car rental in UAE
- Real estate services in Dubai
- Legal services in UAE

3. City + category long-tail pages
- Best [category] in [city]
- [city] [service] near me style pages (without spammy phrasing)

4. Guide pages
- How to find trusted businesses in UAE
- Best service providers by city/category

## Page Template
- Title: Primary keyword | UAEBizConnect
- Meta: concise value proposition + location + trust signal
- H1: exact page intent
- Intro: 150–300 words with natural keyword use
- Internal links: 3–6 related pages
- Optional FAQ: 3–5 high-intent questions
